About Me

The Person
Behind the Campaigns

Y

I'm an email marketing professional based in Nigeria, with about 2 years of experience running campaigns across Substack, Mailchimp, and Canva. I started by building my own newsletter from scratch - which meant learning every part of the email lifecycle by doing it, not studying it.

My approach is content-first: I believe the best email strategy in the world falls apart if the writing doesn't earn the reader's attention. So I obsess over subject lines, take CTAs seriously, and treat every send as a conversation, not a broadcast.
